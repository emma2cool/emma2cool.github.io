Fugglers is just another way to say "funny ugly monsters"! Fugglers are deliberatley ugly-cute plush toys that have human like teeth, and weird facial expressions. They were originally created in the UK by Louise McGettrick in 2010.

WORD GOT AROUND

They were first handmade after she found fake denture teeth on eBay and decided to sew them onto plush animals. She continued to make these plush animals and sold them on Esty. As time went on they continued to grab more peoples attention from being posted on social media, from people creating memes with them, and simply just from one telling another.

THE EXPANSION

In 2018 a Canadian toy company, Spin Master, had purchased the rights to continue producing and creating the plush animals named Fugglers. this brought them into big stores and gace the brand official global visibility.
In 2021 British toy maker Addo Play became the official license holder of the Fugglers brand and helped expand with newer characters and series.
Now, since 2024, the brand has continued to evolve and create partnerships with other known brands such as ZURU. They also continued to created with collaborations to make themed drops and more!
