If you couldn't tell by now, through the evolution of Fugglers we have different versions of them. Starting from the OG's to the special editions.

  1. OG "Fugglers"

    2. These are the originals, the ones Louise McGettrick made herself. They have real human-style denture teeth in them, mismassed limbs, and are what was the start of "Fugglers".

  2. Baby Fuggs

    3. These are smaller, rounder, softer colored, and have less aggressive faces. They usually come with blankets, bottles, or have little diapers on. You can often find them in Five Below.

  3. Keyrings / Minis

    4. These are more pocket sized, you can clip them to your bags or your keys. They are some of the more simpler ones, if you may.

  4. Special Editions

    5. These usually are the holiday themed ones, the ones with costumes, or the ones that are gold and shiny. Some are store exclusives and only last for a short period of time. These are the ones most collectors are looking for to buy.
