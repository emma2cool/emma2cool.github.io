There are so many different types of Fugglers! Below is going to be a list of all the different types of Fugglers:

  1. Different Sizes

    2. Fugglers come in all different sizes. They range from 3.5 inches to 18 inches. A baby Fuggler is 3.5 inches, a keychain Fuggler which is 5 inches, the standard Fuggler is 9 inches, the larger Fuggler is 12 inches, and the giant Fugglers are 18 inches.

  2. Teeth Styles

    3. Fugglers also have different teeth layouts. Some have a full mouth of teeth, compared to other that may only have one. Some may have molers, while others may only have front teeth. Some may have a mix of teeth and others may not. All fugglers are different and unique to each other.

  3. Colors

    4. Fugglers also have lots of unique colors! They have plain colored Fugglers, they have ones where their body color and their hair color are different, they have some that are tie-dye, they have some that have a belly like a penguin, some are sparkly and glitters, and some are shiny. There is such a variety of colors!

  4. Themed

    5. Fugglers may also have a theme to them based on collaborators! There are Ninja Turtle themed Fugglers, Liberta themed Fugglers, Spongebob themed Fugglers, . Along with this, there are also holiday themed Fugglers and more!
